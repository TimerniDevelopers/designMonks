SEO & Content Strategy (AEO & GEO focus)

Traditional SEO focuses on ranking your website in the "blue links" of search engine results pages (like Google). AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) are advanced strategies designed to ensure your brand is the primary source cited by AI models like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google Gemini. While SEO gets you found on a page, AEO/GEO ensures you are the "answer" provided by AI.

SEO is a cumulative strategy. While technical indexing happens within days, significant growth in organic traffic and authority typically takes 6 to 12 months of consistent posting. However, because we optimize for AEO, your content may appear in AI-generated answers and "featured snippets" much sooner than traditional rankings would allow.

GenZ offers a completely hands-off, full-service solution. Our in-house content specialists handle everything from deep-dive research to professional drafting and SEO optimization. We only ask for your brand guidelines and an initial discovery session to ensure the "voice" of the content perfectly reflects your business.

Our process is data-driven and multi-layered. We analyze:

  • Search Intent: What your customers are actually looking for.
  • Competitor Gaps: Where your rivals are weak.
  • Conversational Trends: The specific questions people ask AI and voice assistants.
  • High-Value Keywords: Terms with high conversion potential rather than just high traffic volume.
